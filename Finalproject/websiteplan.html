Commissionary House: Your Trusted Platform for Renting Homes Easily

Site Name

Commissionary House

Commissionary House is a digital platform designed to connect tenants with available rental properties in their area. Users can easily search for houses or apartments, view details and images, and pay rent securely every month—all from one place. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a family, Commissionary House simplifies the rental experience with transparency and convenience.

Site Purpose

The website will:

  • Provide a digital platform that connects tenants with available rental properties.
  • Enable users to search for houses or apartments, view property details and images, and pay rent securely—all in one centralized location.
  • Offer a transparent and convenient experience tailored for students, professionals, and families seeking rental housing.

Scenarios

  • A university student logs into Commissionary House to find an affordable apartment near campus.
  • They filter listings by price, distance, and availability.
  • They view photos and details, then contact the landlord or apply directly

Color Schema

  • #2d4a6a – Used for headers and navigation background (secondary color for branding).
  • #f9f9f9 – Used as the main background color (primary).
  • #333 – Used for main text (body content).
  • white – Used as accent color for text/icons over dark areas.
  • #474091 – Used for section headings (overrides accent for headings).

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headers and navigation (bold, modern feel).
  • Roboto – Used for body text (clean, highly readable).
